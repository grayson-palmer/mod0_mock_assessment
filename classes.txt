Class: Plant

Attributes:
- waterLevel(float)
- minWaterLevel(float)
- height(float)
- potType(string)
- blooming(boolean)
- datePlanted(datetime)

Method:
- flowerBlooming(Sets the blooming attribute to true or false)
- repot(Changes the potType of the plant)
- plantGrowth(Changes the height of the plant)
- waterPlant(Add water to plant when waterLevel gets below minWaterLevel)
